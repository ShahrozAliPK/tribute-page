Mom & Dad

The Most Precious Thing On the Planet
Mother & Father love for the family.

By Syed Shehroz Ali

This is my tribute to all the parents around the globe who have worked very hard just for the sake of their children to be nicest person on the Earth.The dedication of the parents for their continuous hardwork till their last breath is speechless, I mean no power on the Earth could be able to compete with their willingness for the goals they decided to accomplish for their child.After becoming parents, the emotions and feelings of both mother and father are changed, they try to provide everything to their child literally everything! Their goals,dreams,career,mind-set,feelings and bondings have moved to something big which can't be described by the parents themselves.

From toys to pencil till clothing, parents try to provide every luxury to their child as far as they can and as much faster as they can, I mean if travelling faster than speed of light would be possible than surely parents won't stop moving with such a speed so they can quickly get back to their child to hand him over his favourite toy which he demanded just a few seconds back.

There is no end to conclusion on this topic as it is the most valuable and priceless topic to discuss.No scientist, physicist, mathematician, doctor, pilot, business man etc can compete with the level of hardwork and patience which parents possesses.I mean, literally no one! Think about a second how far and long can you stop yourself from not taking a single bite of some super tasty crunch burger spiced with sauces and chillies lying in front of your table and you are in a great hunger for about 8 hours? This is just a glimpse of what and how parents keep their lips compressed and not saying a single word as it could ruin the craviness and hunger of their child.

So, before the page ends up, I would like to end my topic with the following quote for all the parents around the globe who have done more than being a PARENT

"A Father's goodness is higher than the mountains.A Mother's goodness is deeper than the sea."

- Japnese Proverb
